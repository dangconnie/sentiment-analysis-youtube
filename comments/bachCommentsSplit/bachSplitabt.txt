Bach the irrevocable genius. He lives on undying through his music. Isn't it ironic to think the man was considered old-fashioned even boring in his day? But Bach's is a story of redemption. For you see time favored Bach's creative wisdom and so granted his music eternal life. His critics - the names that time forgot.\n\nAlways remember the message of Johann Sebastian Bach: Explore your genius for there lies the key to your immortality.
